Mental Health (19/02/2022)

A platform that brought young people to give their thoughts on mental health and experiences over the same. A handful of young people turned up at Karura forest for the event organized by Greenspace. Where the attendees and participants deliberated on mental health as a point of concern in the society that requires keen attention when need arises.

Tree planting (26/03/2022)

The event was aimed at promoting tree coverage that resulted in friends, partners and communities turning up to be part of the exercise. We had moments of engagements & awareness with the community and attendees about the climate crisis and worthy courses to be done. Unanimous agreements involving communities at grassroots levels in environmental conservation.

Read More

Youth Climate and Governance Summit (30/4/2022)

The summit aimed at bringing together young people delving into youth, climate and governance affairs. Bringing together various stakeholders in climate aversion responses. Promoting the youth to anchor themselves, become a voice of hope in the nation and innovators in their spheres of influence. Outcome - Resolution on climate and governance was arrived at. Engagements of various stakeholders in climate action were achieved as young people got to build capacity and become promoters of peace in the forthcoming elections.

River Clean-up (3/6/2022)

The exercise was conducted by Green rangers safaris which brought together different umbrella bodies championing for environmental safety and learning institutions too. Aimed at promoting clean up exercise at Mbagathi river and southern By-pass that saw collection of many litters clean-up. Helped in commemorating world environment day in style.
